The History of Soccer: How the Game Has Changed Over Time

The history of association of football,more commonly known as football or soccer, streches back at least to medieval times. FIFA(Federation Institute of Football Association) cites Cuju in ancient China is the earliest form of kicking game for which there is scientific evidence,a military manual from the Han Dynasty.and it closely resembles modern football. Similar games were played in ancient Japan, Greece,and Rome. The first written evidence of football match came in England in about 1170.

The development of association football has its origins in medieval ball games and English public school games. The modern game of football originated in the mid-nineteenth century by the efforts of English football clubs to standardize the varying sets of football rules.more history
