Top Players of our Generation

Cristiano Ronaldo

To many Cristiano Ronaldo is regarded as the GOAT (greatest of all time), won lot of individual award and alos major trophy for both is club side and country.
Cristiano Ronaldo has been awarded the best European player 5 times in his career, and has won the European Champions League 6 times. He is the leading goal Scorer in the sport with over 900 goals and still counting.

Lionel Messi

The debate still on who is the best player between Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi, Lionel Messi has won the best player award 8times, making him the most decorated player to ever grace the sport, He helped Argentina secure the last World cup trophy. He is one of the best player in the sport, A living Legend.

Mbappe is the fans favorite at the moment, a very prolific goal scorer with a very technical ability.
